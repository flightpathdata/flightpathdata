Enterprise Data Preboarding

Your data lake, data warehouse, and applications deserve a data partner they can trust.

FlightPath makes it easy to protect downstream data consumers using the Collect Store Validate Publish architecture and CsvPath Framework. As a purpose-built tool, FlightPath is the fastest way to get the efficiency of data preboarding automation.
